Horseshoe Resort to the North from Toronto
Horseshoe Resort is a top Ontario ski and golf resort offering four seasons of fun, conveniently located just over an hour north of Toronto and 20 minutes from Barrie. Experience Horseshoe this Winter, with more than ever to offer adventure seekers and families. Spend time skiing and snowboarding this winter! Fly down our snow tubing hill, hop on a fat bike, lace up your skates and go for a spin on our slopeside skating rink or strap on some snowshoes or cross country skis and explore in the beauty of the Copeland Forest. While you are here, enjoy our delicious dining options and pamper yourself in our full service Amba Spa. Ideal for fun-filled family vacations, year-round getaways and meetings.
